Welcome to the Movie Catalogue database

This site allows you to keep track of your physical movie collection such as DVDs, Blu-Ray and VHS tapes.

  • Not a member? Register a new account now, or login with an existing account.
  • Already registered? Add your first move to your catalogue! You can either fill in the details manually or use the IMDb search function to automatically fill in the details for you.
  • All up to date? View your collection or search for a specific movie.
